# Activity Sticker

Transform your athletic achievements into stylish graphics for Instagram and more. With this small web tool you can import training data from Google Health or Fitbit directly in your browser and generate cool personalized stickers.

### Features

* Complete local processing in the browser without external servers
* Transparent background for perfect overlays on social media
* Customizable layouts, colors and line widths
* Dynamic scaling for the optimal image format

### How it works

1. Export your workout from your fitness app as a TCX file.
2. Open the [web tool](https://its-tom.github.io/tcx-activity-sticker/) in your browser.
3. Upload the file, choose your design and save the final image.

Have fun sharing your athletic milestones!
